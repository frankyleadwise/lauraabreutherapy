Common Questions

Virtual therapy in Tampa — FAQ

Do you have an office in Tampa I can visit?

+

My practice is fully virtual — all sessions are held via secure video. This means no commute, no parking, and the ability to meet from your home, car, or office. Many Tampa clients actually prefer it this way.

Why do you specialize in millennial women?

+

Millennial women face a very specific set of pressures — career ambition, relationship expectations, social media comparison, delaying or navigating motherhood, and the cultural message that you should be thriving. I work with women in their mid-20s to early 40s who are high-functioning but struggling beneath the surface.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person therapy?

+

Yes — research consistently shows telehealth therapy is just as effective as in-person for the issues I treat, including anxiety, relationship issues, and life transitions. Many clients find the comfort of their own space actually helps them open up more quickly.

How quickly can I get started?

+

The free 15-minute consultation can typically be scheduled within a few days. From there, we'll set up your first full session. I keep my caseload intentional so clients don't wait weeks to be seen.

What if I move or travel outside Tampa?

+

As long as you're physically located in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont during a session, we can meet. If you travel out of state temporarily, we'd pause until you return — but your spot stays yours.

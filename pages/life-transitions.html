Life Transitions Therapy

Change can be beautiful. It can also be terrifying.

Major life changes — even the ones we choose — can stir up anxiety, grief, loss of identity, and a profound sense of being unmoored. You don't have to navigate that alone.

Virtual therapy for women navigating life\'s biggest transitions, in Florida, New Jersey, and Vermont — with dedicated support for women in Tampa, Orlando, and St. Petersburg.

Divorce or separation

Moving to a new city or state
Becoming a mother

Coming into a new identity
Empty nest / children leaving home
Health diagnosis (yours or a loved one's)

"Every ending carries within it the seed of something new. Therapy helps you grieve what's gone and step into what's next — with clarity and courage."

What Therapy Gives You

Finding your footing again

Clarity

Untangling what you're actually feeling from the noise of anxiety and fear, so you can make decisions from a grounded place.

Grief Processing

New Identity

Support in reconnecting with who you are — beyond your roles, relationships, or the life you thought you'd have.

How It Works

What anxiety therapy actually looks like

I use Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) — the gold standard for anxiety — along with trauma-informed approaches to address the roots of your worry, not just the surface symptoms.

This is not just talking about your problems. This is building the skills and insight to genuinely change how your mind responds to stress, uncertainty, and fear.

Identify Your Patterns

We map the specific thought patterns and triggers fueling your anxiety so you can start to see them — and interrupt them — in real time.

Build Real Tools

You'll leave each session with practical grounding and coping strategies that work in everyday life — not just in the therapy room.

Address the Root

Anxiety often has deeper roots in past experiences or trauma. We go gently and at your pace to address what's truly driving your overwhelm.

Rebuild Confidence

As your anxiety decreases, your sense of self — your trust in your own judgment, your ability to handle uncertainty — grows in its place.

Common Questions

Anxiety therapy FAQ

How long does anxiety therapy take?

+

It varies by person and the complexity of your experience. Many clients feel meaningful improvement within 2–4 months of consistent weekly sessions. After 2–3 weeks together, we'll build a clear treatment plan so you always know what to expect.

Do you prescribe medication for anxiety?

+

As an LCSW, I do not prescribe medication. However, therapy and medication can work very well together. If medication might be beneficial for you, I can provide referrals to a trusted psychiatrist.

Is online therapy as effective as in-person for anxiety?

+

Yes — research consistently shows that online CBT for anxiety is equally effective as in-person therapy. Many clients actually find it easier to open up and do the work from the comfort of their own environment.

What if I've tried therapy before and it didn't work?

+

I hear this often. The therapeutic relationship and the right fit matter enormously. I'd love to hear what you've experienced before and talk about how my approach might be different. That's exactly what the free consultation is for.

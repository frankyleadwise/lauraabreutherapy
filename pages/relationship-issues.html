Questions

Relationship therapy FAQ

Do you do couples therapy?

+

Currently I specialize in individual therapy for women. My focus is helping you understand yourself, your patterns, and your needs — which ultimately makes all your relationships healthier, romantic or otherwise.

How long until I feel better after a breakup?

+

Grief doesn't follow a timeline. But many clients feel significantly more grounded within 6–10 sessions. The goal isn't to rush your healing — it's to make sure you're healing in a direction that serves your future self.

What if I keep getting back together with an ex?

+

This is more common than you think, and it's exactly the kind of pattern therapy is designed to help with. We'll explore what keeps pulling you back — often attachment wounds or anxiety — without judgment, and build your capacity to choose differently.

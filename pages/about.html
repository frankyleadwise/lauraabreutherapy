A little about me

I'm Laura Abreu — bilingual (English/Spanish) Licensed Clinical Social Worker, world traveler, former actress, and someone who deeply believes in the power of healing from the inside out.

Since 2016, I've had the honor of working with women navigating some of life's most tender and complex moments. People enjoy doing therapy with me because we get to the root causes of why you're stuck — and build a clear, actionable plan together. A lot of my clients see real improvement within the first month.

My Approach

Therapy that's honest, tailored, and trauma-informed

I don't believe in one-size-fits-all therapy. I use a blend of evidence-based practices always tailored to your unique story, history, and goals.

My role is to create a safe, non-judgmental space where you can finally exhale, process your emotions, and begin to heal. Together, we explore the roots of your anxiety, rewrite unhelpful patterns, and build the tools you need to feel grounded and resilient.

Start Your Journey →

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) — Identifying and reshaping thought patterns that fuel anxiety and self-doubt.

Trauma-Focused CBT (TF-CBT) — Specialized approach for processing and healing past trauma at your pace.

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT) — Skills-based techniques for emotional regulation, distress tolerance, and interpersonal effectiveness.

Anti-Oppressive Practice — Therapy that acknowledges the real-world impact of systemic oppression on mental health.

Perinatal Mental Health (PMH-C Certified) — Specialized support for the emotional journey of conception, pregnancy, and postpartum.

Clear Treatment Plans — After 2–3 weeks, we'll build a structured plan together so you always know where we're headed.

Honest Communication — I'll always be clear and direct with you throughout each step of your healing process.

Beyond the Office

A little more about the human behind the LCSW

Before dreaming of becoming a therapist, I was an actress — I grew up in the performing arts and attended the renowned Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School for Performing Arts in New York City. That background shapes how I connect with clients today: I believe storytelling, listening deeply, and creating space for authentic expression are at the heart of healing.

I've traveled to over 30 countries and counting. Immersing myself in different cultures has deepened my belief that every person's experience is shaped by context, history, and community — and that perspective is something I carry into every therapy room.

I currently live in Tampa, Florida with my husband, daughter, and two cats. When I'm not working, you'll find me at the beach, hiking, kayaking, or trying a new restaurant or recipe at home.

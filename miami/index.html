Common Questions

Online therapy in Miami — FAQ

Do you offer in-person sessions in Miami?

+

My practice is fully virtual. All sessions are held via secure, HIPAA-compliant video. Miami clients love not having to deal with Brickell parking or the Palmetto at rush hour — you can log on from anywhere in Florida.

Do you work with Latina or bilingual women?

+

Yes. Miami's cultural diversity is one of its greatest strengths. I work with women from all backgrounds and understand the unique pressures that come with navigating identity, family expectations, and culture in South Florida.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Yes — research consistently shows telehealth therapy is just as effective as in-person for anxiety, relationship issues, and life transitions. Many clients find the comfort of their own space helps them open up faster.

What if I travel between Miami and another city?

+

As long as you're physically in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont during your session, we can meet. If you travel out of state temporarily, we'd pause until you return.

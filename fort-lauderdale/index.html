Common Questions

Online therapy in Fort Lauderdale — FAQ

Do you have an office in Fort Lauderdale or Broward?

+

My practice is fully virtual. Broward County traffic is notoriously brutal — skip it entirely and log on from anywhere in Florida.

Do you work with women across all of Broward County?

+

Yes — I see women across all of Broward County, from the beach to the western suburbs, all via telehealth.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Yes — research consistently shows telehealth therapy is just as effective as in-person for anxiety, relationship issues, and life transitions. Many clients find the comfort of their own space helps them open up faster.

What if I split time between Fort Lauderdale and Miami?

+

As long as you're physically in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont during your session, we can meet. If you travel out of state temporarily, we'd pause until you return.

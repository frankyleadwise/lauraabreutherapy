Common Questions

Online therapy in Jacksonville — FAQ

Do you have an office in Jacksonville?

+

My practice is fully virtual. All sessions are held via secure, HIPAA-compliant video. Jacksonville's size makes commuting to therapy tough — log on from anywhere in Florida instead.

Do you work with military families or veterans?

+

Yes. Jacksonville has a significant military community, and I understand the unique stressors that come with deployments, relocations, and life as a military spouse or partner.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Yes — research consistently shows telehealth therapy is just as effective as in-person for anxiety, relationship issues, and life transitions. Many clients find the comfort of their own space helps them open up faster.

What if I relocate or travel for work?

+

As long as you're physically in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont during your session, we can meet. If you travel out of state temporarily, we'd pause until you return.

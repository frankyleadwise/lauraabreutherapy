Common Questions

Oxford Health therapy — FAQ

Does Oxford Health cover virtual therapy?

+

Yes. Oxford Health Plans covers telehealth mental health services including therapy with licensed clinical social workers. Laura Abreu, LCSW is in-network with Oxford Health in Florida, New Jersey, and Vermont.

What is the difference between Oxford and UnitedHealthcare?

+

Oxford Health Plans is a subsidiary of UnitedHealthcare that operates primarily in the Northeast. If your insurance card says Oxford Health or Oxford Health Plans, Laura is in-network for your mental health benefits.

What will I pay per session with Oxford Health?

+

Your cost depends on your specific Oxford plan's copay and deductible. Call the member services number on your insurance card and ask about your behavioral health or mental health benefits for telehealth services.

Can I use Oxford Health insurance for online therapy in Florida?

+

Yes. Oxford Health covers telehealth therapy sessions with in-network providers in Florida. You just need to be physically located in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont at the time of your session.

Common Questions

Aetna therapy — FAQ

Does Aetna cover virtual therapy in Florida?

+

Yes. Aetna covers telehealth therapy in Florida, including mental health services from licensed therapists like Laura Abreu, LCSW. The exact coverage depends on your specific Aetna plan.

What is my copay for therapy with Aetna?

+

Copays vary by plan — they typically range from $0 to $50 per session depending on your specific Aetna plan, deductible status, and whether you've met your out-of-pocket maximum. The best way to know your exact cost is to call Aetna directly or log into your member portal.

Do I need a referral to see a therapist with Aetna?

+

In most cases, no. Most Aetna plans allow you to see an in-network mental health provider directly without a referral. However, we recommend calling Aetna to confirm your specific plan's requirements.

What Aetna plans does Laura Abreu accept?

+

Laura accepts most Aetna commercial plans including Aetna Choice POS II, Aetna Select, and Aetna Open Access. If you're unsure whether your specific plan is covered, reach out and we'll help you verify.

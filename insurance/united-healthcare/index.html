Common Questions

United Healthcare therapy — FAQ

Does United Healthcare cover virtual therapy?

+

Yes. UnitedHealthcare covers telehealth mental health services including therapy with licensed clinical social workers like Laura Abreu, LCSW in Florida, New Jersey, and Vermont.

What does UHC pay for therapy sessions?

+

Your cost depends on your specific UHC plan's copay, deductible, and whether you've met your out-of-pocket maximum. Most UHC members pay $10–$50 per session after the deductible is met. Log into myuhc.com to check your specific benefits.

Does Laura accept UHC and Optum?

+

Laura is in-network with United Healthcare (UHC) and UnitedHealthcare UBH. If your card references Optum Behavioral Health, please reach out so we can verify your specific plan.

How do I find out my UHC copay for therapy?

+

The quickest way is to call the member services number on the back of your card and ask about your mental health or behavioral health benefits for telehealth services with an in-network LCSW.

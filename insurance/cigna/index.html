Common Questions

Cigna therapy — FAQ

Does Cigna cover online therapy in Florida?

+

Yes. Cigna covers telehealth mental health services in Florida, including sessions with licensed therapists via video. Laura Abreu is in-network with Cigna and Evernorth.

What is my copay for Cigna therapy sessions?

+

Copays vary by plan. Most Cigna members pay a similar amount for therapy as they would for a specialist visit. Log into myCigna.com or call the member number on your card to find your exact copay.

Is Evernorth the same as Cigna for mental health?

+

Yes — Evernorth is Cigna's behavioral health division. If your insurance card says Evernorth or Cigna Behavioral Health, Laura is in-network for your mental health benefits.

How do I use my Cigna benefits with Laura?

+

Simply reach out through our Get Started page. Laura's team will verify your Cigna benefits before your first session so you know exactly what to expect cost-wise.

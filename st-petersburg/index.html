Common Questions

Online therapy in St. Petersburg — FAQ

Do you have an office in St. Pete or Pinellas County?

+

My practice is fully virtual. Pinellas County traffic — especially over the bridges — can be brutal. Skip it and log on from anywhere in Florida.

Do you work with transplants and newcomers to St. Pete?

+

Absolutely. St. Pete draws a lot of transplants — women who moved for a fresh start and are still figuring out who they are in a new city. That transition is real, and therapy helps.

Is virtual therapy as effective as in-person?

+

Yes — research consistently shows telehealth therapy is just as effective as in-person for anxiety, relationship issues, and life transitions. Many clients find the comfort of their own space helps them open up faster.

What if I split time between St. Pete and Tampa?

+

As long as you're physically in Florida, New Jersey, or Vermont during your session, we can meet. If you travel out of state temporarily, we'd pause until you return.

Meet Your Therapist

Hey, I'm Laura — and I get it.

Since 2016, I've walked alongside women through their most tender, complicated, and transformative moments. People enjoy doing therapy with me because we get to the root causes of why you're stuck — and we build a clear plan to get you moving again.

I'm a bilingual (English/Spanish), BIPOC therapist trained in CBT, Trauma-Focused CBT, DBT, and Psychodynamic Theory. I bring cultural sensitivity, genuine warmth, and evidence-based tools to every session — always in a space free from judgment.

Simple to Start

Three steps to finding peace

Starting therapy shouldn't feel overwhelming. Here's exactly what to expect.

1

Book Your Free Call

Select "Book Now" and fill out a brief intake form. You'll receive a link for a free 15-minute consultation so we can see if we're a good fit.

2

Your First Session

We meet virtually for a 60-minute session to explore your story — your history, current challenges, and what you're hoping to heal and build.
